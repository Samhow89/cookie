Instructions:

  • Click the "Bake Cookie!" button to earn cookies. Each click on the button will increase your cookie count.
  • Use the cookies to hire employees who will automatically generate more cookies for you. Click the "Hire an employee for £10!" button to hire an employee. You need to have at least £10 cash to hire an employee.
  • The more employees you have, the faster you'll earn cookies. Keep increasing your cookie count and hiring more employees to maximize your cookie production!
  • You can also increase the size of your cookies for better selling prices. Click the "Increase cookie size for £50" button to increase the cookie size. This will change the selling price of each cookie.
  • To sell your cookies and earn cash, click the "Sell all cookies" button. The cash you earn will be based on the number of cookies you have and the current selling price per cookie.
  • The store management section displays your current cash balance and the selling price per cookie.

Enjoy the addictive fun of Cookie Clicker and build your cookie empire!
